Upcoming Events

Hackathon

Description: A competitive coding event where students showcase their programming skills and creativity to solve real-world problems.

Goal: Promote teamwork, innovation, and problem-solving in a competitive and fun environment.

Tech Talk Series

Description: Monthly seminars or webinars featuring industry professionals, researchers, and alumni discussing current trends, technologies, and career insights in computer science.

Goal: Provide students with exposure to industry knowledge and networking opportunities.

Research Symposium

Description: An event showcasing research projects and papers by faculty and students, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Goal: Highlight the department's research contributions and encourage interdisciplinary collaboration.

Industry Networking Night

Description: An evening event bringing together students, faculty, and industry professionals for networking, mentorship, and potential internship/job opportunities. Strengthen ties between academia and industry

Code for Good Community Project

A community outreach program where students collaborate on a coding project that addresses a local social or environmental issue.Apply technical skills to make a positive impact on the community while fostering teamwork and social responsibility.

AI and Machine Learning Workshop:

Description: A hands-on workshop exploring the practical applications of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Goal: Enhance students' understanding of cutting-edge technologies and their practical implementation.

Cybersecurity Awareness Week

Description: A series of events, including workshops, talks, and demonstrations, focused on raising awareness about cybersecurity threats and best practices. Goal: Educate students and the community on the importance of cybersecurity in today's digital landscape.

Start-up Pitch Competition

Description: An entrepreneurial event where students present innovative tech start-up ideas to a panel of judges. Goal: Encourage entrepreneurial spirit, creativity, and business acumen among students.

Women in Tech Panel Discussion

Description: A panel discussion featuring successful women in the tech industry, sharing their experiences and insights. Goal: Promote diversity and inclusion in the field of computer science and inspire female students to pursue careers in technology.

Open Source Contribution Day

Description: A collaborative event where students contribute to open-source projects, fostering a sense of community and giving back to the global tech community. Goal: Encourage students to engage with the wider programming community and develop practical skills.

Blockchain Technology Seminar

Description: A seminar exploring the principles, applications, and challenges of blockchain technology. Goal: Educate students on emerging technologies and their potential impact on various industries.

Game Development Workshop

Description: A hands-on workshop guiding students through the basics of game development, from design to coding. Goal: Introduce students to the world of game development and inspire creativity in interactive digital media.
